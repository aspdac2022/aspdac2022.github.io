The registration fee includes:

PAYMENT

All registration fees must be paid in NT dollars by credit card only. Please note that personal checks, bank drafts and bank transfer will not be accepted. Payments are processed via our secure gateway through Hua Nan Bank. Your credit card statement will show a debit for this charge to "Poetry-Life Professional Conference Organizer".
You are NOT able to download the conference proceeding or watch the pre-recorded presentation if you did not finish the payment. Please finish the payment within a week after you registered (finish the payment immediately if you register on January). The receipt is available on the registration site after your payment is complete.

The following credit cards will be accepted:
VISA, MasterCard, JCB

PAYMENT OF ADDITIONAL PAGE CHARGE

The limit to the number of pages per paper can be extended up to eight (8) by paying an additional charge for pages beyond six (6). The details of the page limit policy and charge can be found here.
If you need to pay an additional page charge, you can pay it with registration fee from the registration site. Please select the total number of pages whose charge is paid by you of your papers.

CANCELLATION AND REFUND

When written notification of cancellation after you paid is received by the conference secretariat by December 31, 2021, NT$ 750 will be deducted from the fees paid to cover administrative costs. No refunds will be made for cancellation requests received after this date. Speakers are not allowed to cancel registrations.
